# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: October 09, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to PiqDrop. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy applies to both the PiqDrop sender application and PiqDrop Rider delivery application (collectively "the Apps"). This policy will inform you about how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our Apps, and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

**Age Requirement:** You must be at least 18 years old to use PiqDrop services. By using our Apps, you confirm that you are 18 years of age or older.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of personal data:

### 2.1 Information You Provide
* **Identity Data:** First name, last name, username or similar identifier
* **Contact Data:** Email address and telephone numbers  
* **Profile Data:** Username, password (encrypted), profile picture, and identity verification documents (for riders)
* **Location Data:** Pickup and delivery addresses, current location when using the app
* **Payment Information:** Payment card details (processed securely by Stripe - see section 4)
* **Photos:** Package photos for documentation purposes (via camera or photo library)
* **Vehicle Information:** (For riders) Vehicle type, registration details

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically
* **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
* **Usage Data:** How you interact with our Apps, features used, time spent
* **Location Data:** When you use location-based features (with your permission)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
* **Google Sign-In:** If you use Google to sign in, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google
* **Stripe:** Payment processing information and transaction status

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Service Delivery
* Create and manage your account
* Process delivery requests and payments
* Match senders with available riders
* Facilitate communication between senders and riders
* Provide customer support
* Send service-related notifications (delivery updates, account changes)

### 3.2 Location Data Usage
* **For Senders:** Display your current location to set pickup addresses, show nearby riders
* **For Riders:** Display available deliveries near you, provide navigation to pickup/delivery locations
* **Note:** Location data is collected only when the app is in use (foreground). We do NOT collect background location data or track you when the app is closed.

### 3.3 Photos and Camera Access
* Capture package photos for delivery documentation
* Upload profile pictures
* Document package condition for delivery proof
* All photos are stored securely and only accessible to relevant parties (sender, rider, support team)

### 3.4 Legal Basis for Processing
We process your data based on:
* **Contract Performance:** To provide our delivery services
* **Legitimate Interest:** To improve our services and prevent fraud
* **Legal Obligation:** To comply with tax and financial regulations
* **Consent:** When required by law, such as for location access

## 4. Payment Processing & Financial Data

### 4.1 Stripe Payment Processing
* All payment transactions are processed by **Stripe, Inc.**, our third-party payment processor
* We do NOT store your complete credit/debit card information on our servers
* Stripe handles all payment card data in compliance with PCI-DSS standards
* We receive only transaction confirmations and payment status from Stripe
* Stripe's privacy policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

### 4.2 Payment Data We Store
* Transaction history (amount, date, status)
* Last 4 digits of card for reference
* Payment receipts

### 4.3 Rider Payouts
* Riders receive earnings through Stripe Connect
* Bank account information is stored and processed by Stripe
* We do not have direct access to full bank account details

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

### 5.1 Stripe (Payment Processing)
* **Purpose:** Process payments and rider payouts
* **Data Shared:** Name, email, payment information, transaction details
* **Privacy Policy:** https://stripe.com/privacy

### 5.2 Google Sign-In (Authentication)
* **Purpose:** Allow you to sign in with your Google account
* **Data Shared:** Name, email address, profile picture
* **Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 5.3 Google Maps (Navigation & Location)
* **Purpose:** Display maps, provide navigation, show locations
* **Data Shared:** Location coordinates, addresses
* **Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 6. How We Share Your Information

We share your personal data only in the following circumstances:

### 6.1 Within the Platform
* **Senders see:** Rider's name, profile picture, vehicle info, live location during active delivery
* **Riders see:** Sender's name, pickup/delivery addresses, package details, contact info
* **Both parties:** Can communicate through in-app messaging

### 6.2 With Third Parties
* **Payment Processors:** Stripe for payment processing
* **Service Providers:** Cloud hosting, data storage, analytics (with strict confidentiality agreements)
* **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or government request

### 6.3 We DO NOT:
* Sell your personal data to third parties
* Share your data for marketing purposes without consent
* Provide your data to advertisers

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

* **Encryption:** All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses SSL/TLS encryption
* **Secure Storage:** Personal data is stored on secure servers with restricted access
* **Password Protection:** Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing
* **Payment Security:** All payment data is handled by PCI-DSS compliant Stripe
* **Access Controls:** Only authorized personnel can access personal data
* **Regular Audits:** We conduct security audits and updates regularly

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Data Retention

### 8.1 Retention Periods
* **Active Accounts:** We retain your data while your account is active
* **After Account Deletion:** Most data is deleted within 30 days
* **Legal Requirements:** Some data (transaction records, tax documents) may be retained for 7 years to comply with legal obligations
* **Anonymized Data:** We may retain anonymized analytics data indefinitely

### 8.2 What Happens When You Delete Your Account
* Profile information: Deleted within 30 days
* Delivery history: Anonymized (names/contact removed)
* Financial records: Retained for legal compliance (7 years)
* Photos: Deleted within 30 days
* Messages: Deleted within 30 days

## 9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 9.1 Access & Portability
* **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
* **Data Portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

### 9.2 Correction & Deletion
* **Right to Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data
* **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)

### 9.3 Control & Objection
* **Right to Object:** Object to processing of your data for certain purposes
* **Right to Restriction:** Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
* **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time (where consent is the legal basis)

### 9.4 How to Exercise Your Rights
* **In-App:** Go to Settings > Privacy > Manage Data
* **Email:** support@piqdrop.com
* **Data Deletion API:** https://piqdrop.com/api/data-deletion
* **Response Time:** We will respond within 30 days

## 10. Location Data & Permissions

### 10.1 Location Permission Types
* **"When Using the App" (Foreground):** Used for:
  - Setting pickup/delivery addresses
  - Showing your location on map
  - Finding nearby riders/deliveries
  - Navigation during active delivery

### 10.2 Location Data Storage
* We store addresses you enter for delivery purposes
* Historical locations are not tracked or stored
* You can revoke location permission in device settings (app functionality will be limited)

### 10.3 No Background Tracking
* We do NOT track your location when the app is closed or in the background
* Location is only accessed when you actively use location features
* You have full control through device settings

## 11. Photos & Camera Access

### 11.1 Why We Need Camera/Photo Access
* **Package Photos:** Document package condition for delivery proof
* **Profile Pictures:** Optional profile customization
* **Identity Verification:** For rider account verification (one-time)

### 11.2 Photo Storage & Access
* Photos are stored on secure servers
* Only relevant parties can view (sender, assigned rider, support team)
* You can delete photos from your delivery history
* We do not access your photo library beyond images you explicitly select

## 12. Children's Privacy

Our services are NOT intended for anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@piqdrop.com, and we will delete such information immediately.

## 13. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy and applicable laws.

## 14. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:
* Right to know what personal data is collected
* Right to know if personal data is sold or disclosed
* Right to opt-out of sale of personal data (we do not sell data)
* Right to deletion of personal data
* Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@piqdrop.com

## 15. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR including:
* Right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data
* Right to data portability
* Right to withdraw consent
* Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

## 16. Data Deletion Requests

### 16.1 How to Request Deletion
You can request deletion of your account and all associated data:

**Method 1: In-App**
1. Open the app
2. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
3. Confirm deletion request

**Method 2: API Request**
Send a DELETE request to:
```
https://piqdrop.com/api/data-deletion
```
Include your registered email address in the request body.

**Method 3: Email**
Email us at: support@piqdrop.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"

### 16.2 Deletion Timeline
* Account deactivation: Immediate
* Data deletion: Within 30 days
* Confirmation email: Sent when deletion is complete
* Legal retention data: Retained only as required by law
